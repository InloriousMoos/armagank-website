Bachelor's & Master's topics

I regularly supervise Bachelor's and Master's students on the following topics. Students are welcome to propose related topics as well.

  • Enhancing physical well-being, exercise and sporting experiences through design
  • User experience in digital health and wellbeing
  • Human-centred design, UX, experience design and UI design
  • Design and behaviour change
  • Personal informatics and self-tracking
